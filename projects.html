H2OhNo!

H2OhNo is an innovative solution to combat water contamination in developing regions. Can detect if water is contaminated based on user's water sample and user descriptions. Finally, H2OhNo includes a virtual mapping feature where contamination reports are plotted to help identify clusters of contamination reports in the same area.
